About Us

Distroset, established in 2023, is a small yet warmly receptive music distributor that prides itself on being independent and artist-led. Founded by a collective of dedicated musicians, Distroset offers a platform for independent artists seeking a community-focused and artist-centered distribution experience. We offer:


Embracing a friendly and personable approach, Distroset values the unique voices and talents of individual artists. Distroset aims to empower artists by enabling them to maintain control over their work and reach wider audiences while retaining their artistic integrity.


Join Distroset, and become part of a vibrant community where artists support each other and forge lasting connections that transcend geographical boundaries.
